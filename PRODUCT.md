# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Two audiences share one page:

- **Patients and their families**, often older adults who have just been referred for a cardiac concern and may be anxious. Their context is a phone or laptop at home, wanting reassurance, clear next steps, and an easy way to book or find the rooms.
- **Referring GPs and specialists**, who need to quickly gauge the team's credibility and find how to send a referral and reach reception.

The jobs to be done: understand what the practice offers, trust the people who will provide care, and act (call to book, or request a referral form).

## Product Purpose

The marketing site for GC HeartCare, a private cardiology practice at Gold Coast Private Hospital, Southport. It reproduces the content and functionality of the legacy gcheartcare.com with a fresher design: practice overview, services, the four cardiologists, sonographers, patient and referrer information, and contact details with a map. Success is a referred patient who feels reassured enough to book, and a referrer who trusts the practice enough to send patients its way.

## Brand Personality

Calm, reassuring, and warm. The practice should feel like a local Gold Coast team that treats people like family, not a corporate clinic. Voice is plain, human, and unhurried: it explains rather than sells, and it carries quiet authority through credentials and care, never through hype.

## Anti-references

- **Generic SaaS / tech landing pages.** No hero-metric templates, no endless identical icon-card grids, no vibrant tech gradients.
- **Loud or salesy marketing.** No buzzwords, urgency banners, or aggressive calls to action. Understatement reads as trustworthy here.
- **Cluttered, busy layouts.** No dense walls of text or overstimulating sections. Generous, calm space is part of the reassurance.

## Design Principles

- **Calm over clever.** The primary job is to lower a worried patient's heart rate, not to impress. Clarity and breathing room beat flourish.
- **Earn the referrer's trust.** Credentials, areas of focus, and the promise of timely reporting are first-class content, not afterthoughts.
- **Local and human.** Write and design like a Gold Coast practice that knows its patients by name. Warmth carried through copy, imagery, and pacing.
- **Quietly premium.** Restraint signals a private specialist practice. Refinement over decoration; never shout.
- **Legible by default.** The audience skews older. Contrast, type size, and reduced-motion support are non-negotiable, not polish.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Body text at or above 4.5:1 contrast (large text at or above 3:1), full keyboard operability, visible focus states, and a reduced-motion alternative for every animation. Type stays comfortably legible for older readers. Colour is never the sole carrier of meaning.
